How to use

Welcome to the AP Chem Ion quiz. When inputting ion formulas, regular numbers (0-9) will be replaced with subscript versions of their normal counterparts (₀-₉ {the lower ones}). You can input superscript numbers (⁰-⁹ {the higher ones}) by holding Shift and the corresponding number key. Plus and minus will also be converted to their superscript counterparts automagically. For cations, the parentheses and Roman numerals are required exactly as they are shown in the names on the ion sheet.
